Overview

Short version: Invofly stores all your data locally on your device. We do not have servers. We do not collect personal data. Your business information never leaves your phone.

Invofly ("we", "our", "the app") is a time tracking and invoicing application for freelancers. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

By using Invofly, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2

Data We Collect

Data you enter directly:

  • Client names, email addresses, phone numbers, company names
  • Time entries, project names, task descriptions
  • Invoice details, amounts, tax and discount values
  • Your business name, address, and contact information

App settings (stored locally):

  • Language preference
  • Dark/light mode preference
  • Default currency
  • Pro license activation status

We do NOT collect: location data, device identifiers, analytics, usage statistics, crash reports, or any personal information beyond what you enter yourself.

3

Data Storage

All data entered in Invofly is stored exclusively on your device using a local SQLite database and device preferences (SharedPreferences). We do not operate any servers or cloud infrastructure to store your data.

This means:

  • Your data is private by default and never transmitted to us
  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all your data
  • We cannot recover your data if your device is lost or reset
  • We recommend exporting CSV reports regularly as a backup
4

Third-Party Services

Invofly uses one third-party service for payment processing only:

Creem.io — Payment processor for Pro license purchases.

  • Used only when you choose to purchase the Pro version
  • Creem.io collects your email and payment information to process the transaction
  • We receive only a license key — no payment card data is shared with us
  • Creem.io's privacy policy: creem.io/privacy

PDF sharing — When you share an invoice as PDF, the sharing is handled by your device's native share sheet (iOS Share Sheet / Android Share). The PDF file is temporarily stored in your device's cache directory and automatically deleted.

5

License Keys

When you activate the Pro version with a license key:

  • The key is validated against Creem.io's API over a secure HTTPS connection
  • The key and activation status are stored locally on your device only
  • License keys are valid for 1 year from the date of activation
  • We do not store a record of which device your key is activated on

Keep your license key safe. You can reuse it to restore Pro access on any device, or after reinstalling the app.

6

Children's Privacy

Invofly is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

7

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Access: All your data is directly accessible inside the app
  • Export: Use the CSV export feature (Pro) to export all your data
  • Delete: Delete individual entries, clients, or invoices within the app at any time
  • Full erasure: Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all data

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to access, rectify, and erase your personal data. Since we do not process or store your personal data on our servers, these rights are exercised directly on your device.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
