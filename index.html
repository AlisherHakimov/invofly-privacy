Privacy Policy

Developer: Alisher Khakimov · App: Invofly · Effective: April 25, 2026

Short version: Invofly stores all data locally on your device. No servers, no analytics, no personal data collection.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the Invofly mobile application developed by Alisher Khakimov. By using Invofly, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Data We Collect

Data you enter directly:

  • Client names, emails, phone numbers, company names
  • Time entries, project names, task descriptions
  • Invoice details, amounts, tax and discount values
  • Your business name, address, contact info

App preferences (stored locally):

  • Language, dark/light mode, default currency
  • Pro license activation status and expiry

We do NOT collect: location, device IDs, crash reports, analytics, or any data beyond what you enter yourself.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored exclusively on your device using a local SQLite database. We operate no servers. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all your data.

4. Third-Party Services

No analytics or advertising SDKs. The only optional third-party interaction is payment processing when purchasing Pro (handled by Creem.io). We receive only a license key — no payment data is shared with us.

5. Children's Privacy

Invofly is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and delete entries directly in the app
  • Export data as CSV (Pro feature)
  • Full erasure: uninstall to permanently delete everything
